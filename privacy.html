Linguax Accessible: Learn Languages

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Educational simulator notice

Linguax Accessible is an educational simulator for learning and preparation only. It is not an official exam platform and does not guarantee any exam result.

Progress data is saved locally in the user's browser.

Linguax Accessible does not collect, sell or share personal data.

Local storage only

Learning progress, selected language, accessibility settings and certificate progress are stored locally on the user's device using browser localStorage.

No account and no backend

The app does not require account creation, does not use a backend server and does not send learning data to Laura Andreea or external services.

No advertising or analytics SDKs

The app does not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs or paid tracking services.

Voice support

Voice features may use the browser or device speech capabilities. No voice data is stored by this app.

Data Safety summary

  • No data is collected by the app.
  • No data is shared with third parties by the app.
  • Progress is stored locally on the user's device.
  • Users can reset local progress from inside the app.
  • The app is an educational simulator and is not an official exam platform.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the project author through GitHub.
